What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies do not identify you personally — they simply recognise your device.

How we use cookies

The Funding Fusion website is a simple static site. We aim to use the minimum number of cookies necessary to make the site function. We do not use advertising cookies, behavioural tracking cookies or any cookies that share your data with third-party advertisers.

Types of cookies we use

Cookie type Purpose Duration
Essential cookies Required for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled as the site would not work without them. Session
Analytics cookies If we use an analytics tool (such as Google Analytics), these cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site — e.g. which pages are most visited. No personally identifiable information is collected. Up to 2 years

We do not currently use any marketing, advertising or social media cookies.

Third-party cookies

Our website uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. Google may set cookies when serving these fonts. We do not control these cookies. You can read Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Managing cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

— View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
— Block third-party cookies
— Block all cookies from specific sites
— Block all cookies from being set
— Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please be aware that deleting or blocking cookies may affect your experience on our website. To find out how to manage cookies in your browser, visit aboutcookies.org.

Your consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of essential cookies as described in this policy. Where we use analytics cookies, we will seek your consent where required by applicable law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page, with the date of the most recent update shown at the top.
